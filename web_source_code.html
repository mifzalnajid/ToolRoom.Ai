How It Works

From drawing to matched supplier
in under a minute.

01 / Upload
Upload Your Drawing
Drop a PDF engineering drawing. The system reads it instantly — no manual data entry, no forms to fill.
02 / Extract
AI Reads the Drawing
AI extracts material, diameter, length, tolerances, surface finish, and all required manufacturing operations from the drawing.
03 / Route
Process Route Card
An ordered manufacturing sequence is generated — turning, boring, milling, heat treatment, grinding — with inhouse or outsource flags.
04 / Match
Supplier Matching
Toolrooms are scored against the part's requirements — process capability, material, dimensions, machine capacity — and ranked.
05 / RFQ
Auto RFQ Dispatch
Matched toolrooms receive structured RFQs instantly with all specs attached. Zero phone calls. Zero manual effort.
06 / Compare
Compare & Decide
All quotes come back to one dashboard. Compare prices against market benchmarks and place the order with confidence.
What It Does

Every feature built for
the machine shop floor.

01 — AI Extraction
Drawing Interpretation
Computer vision reads PDF drawings and extracts all technical specs — material, diameter, length, tolerances, Ra surface finish values, and required operations. No manual input.
02 — Process Planning
Route Card Generation
Manufacturing domain rules produce an ordered operation sequence. CNC turning → boring → VMC milling → heat treatment → grinding → inspection. Each step flagged inhouse or outsource.
03 — Smart Matching
Capability-Based Supplier Match
Toolrooms are scored across process capability, material match, machine capacity, and equipment vintage. The best-fit suppliers for that specific part rise to the top.
04 — Price Intelligence
Quote Benchmarking
Submitted quotes are compared against market pricing data. Know in seconds whether a quoted price is fair, inflated, or a bargain — before you commit to an order.
